                               Augustin Huret - CEO




1991: The French nuclear agency wants to find the root causes of / understand nuclear leakages. Difficulty: very small sample, but a lot of variables. Augustin Huret has the idea to use Alexandre Grothendieck’s algebraic geometry. By using a Cray 2, he cracks the problem.


1993:  Augustin graduate from Polyetchnique in mathematics, computer sciences and nuclear physics


1999: Augustin is the CSO of a Honeywell division. 30% of the turbochargers need balancing and rework. He develops a new version of his algorithm which solves the problem. He launches Pertinence, which specializes in industrial processes. The software company is sold to Dassault Systems.


2005: Augustin launches Hypercube. It applies Augustin’s know-how to behavioral modeling. Part of Hypercube is sold to Société Générale for quantitative trading in 2007, another part to Bearing Point in 2012


2014: A US Federal Agency asks Augustin to design a data analysis software that could be used without training. This is MondoBrain, a breakthrough in a market where “big data” software are reserved to experts and priced accordingly. This is made possible by the exponential increase in computing power offered by cloud computing.

                                 Greg Jennings - CTO


2001: Graduation from Longwood University with a double major in physics and chemistry then University of Virginia with a MBA in Material Science and engineering.


2004: Greg begin working as a senior associate with Booz Allen Hamilton, managing a consulting team. He delivered analytical solutions using research, forecasting and data visulalization.


2011: Greg is the CTO of Decision Forge after being a softaware developer for PBS Learning Media


2013: For the developement of a major Canadian Oil Transportation Company, Greg worked as a consultant for almost 4 years with ApexVis, developing a decision support system.


2014: Using all of his experience as a developer and of support system, Greg lauchned MondoBrain with Augustin, creating the concept of an Augmented Intelligence, compex mathematics that anyone can use to optimize systems.
